Browser audio uses the voices installed on your device, so quality varies by machine. For a big jump, download an enhanced / premium system voice — on Mac: System Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → System Voice → Manage Voices (the “Siri” and “(Enhanced)” voices sound far more human). On iOS it’s Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices. New voices show up in this list automatically. For true podcast-grade narration, see the handoff notes on neural TTS.
